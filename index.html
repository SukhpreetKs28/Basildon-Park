Front exterior view of Basildon House

Basildon Park, located between the villages of Upper Basildon and Lower Basildon in West Berkshire, England, is a Grade I-listed building owned by the National Trust and made popular by its appearances in iconic films and shows such as Bridgerton and the 2005 Pride & Prejudice.
